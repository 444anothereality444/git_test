    Linux

    • Why you should use Linux or BSD OSs

      • Linux and *BSD operating systems are based on unix. According to w3techs, internet is ruled by more than 55% of its servers running with Linux* vs 11% with Windows. Using Linux or BSD as an operating system has at least two advantages. First, in order to use your system, you WILL have to learn commands that are also used in servers*. Also note that starting with no DE* will make it easier for you to learn since all of your commands* will be typed directly into the terminal*. Secondly, you will have to configure your operating system and especially your network*. With only these two elements in mind, you will be able to swim.
